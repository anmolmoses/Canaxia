Blurb

The Tech industry is booming like anything. This world is growing at a record pace. Along with that, people have been blessed with natural-born powers called cosmic powers. Each individual born in that world is affiliated with one of these cosmic powers fire, air, water, and land.

 

The story revolves around our protagonist, named Eric, who one day wakes up to only find out that the fire ability that he possessed is no longer with him making him the first person to not have any typical cosmic power. He goes on a quest to find out the answers to his lost power, only to discover the dark secrets of the world in which he lives and about himself

 

Join me in unfolding the mysteries of the world Canaxia.
