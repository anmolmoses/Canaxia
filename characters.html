Gary

Lost his parents to the Great War, and his sister, who was taking care of him, died shortly after due to an accident at work...At the age of 11, Gary was an orphan who was later adopted by Maya. After a few years of staying with Maya, Gary was banished from the town where he lived, but why? Also, he has a mark on his neck. Does that mark mean something?

“I never asked for anything in life. Yet what I had was taken away from me. But that doesn’t stop me from protecting the one I love. To my last breath, I will protect them no matter what.”
-Gary
